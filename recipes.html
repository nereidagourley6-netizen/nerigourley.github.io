Pad Thai

A bright, zesty dish ready in under 20 minutes! Perfect for busy weeknights.

Ingredients
Ingredient Amount
Rice Vermicelli 8 oz
Red Pepper, Julienne 1 ea
Scallion, cut into 1" pieces 6 each
Peanuts, Chopped 1/2 c
Cilantro Chopped 1/2 c
Bean Sprouts 1 c
Garlic 4 ea
Shallot, sliced 1 ea
Brown Sugar 5 Teaspoons
Fish Sauce 3 Teaspoons
Black Vinegar 1.5 Teaspoons
Siracha 2 Teaspoons
Soy Sauce 1 Teaspoons
Shrimp 8 oz
Chicken Thigh 8 oz
Instructions
Step Description
1 Marinate chicken in small amount of soy.
2 Sear chicken, when browned add shrimp and cook through then set aside.
3 Clean pan and sweat garlic, shallot, and pepper.
4 Push to one side of pan and cook eggs separately.
5 Mix together and add scallion and cilantro, cook until onions soften and add peanuts.
6 Add sauce, then noodles and bean sprouts
