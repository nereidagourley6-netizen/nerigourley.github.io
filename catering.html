Catering Rates & Availability

Our catering services are designed to bring exceptional dining experiences to any occasion, with flexible options to suit both weekdays and weekends. Breakfast catering is available at $25 per person on weekdays and $40 per person on weekends, while lunch catering is offered at $35 per person on weekdays and $55 per person on weekends. For evening events, our dinner catering is priced at $65 per person on weekdays and $85 per person on weekends. Guests can also enhance their event with cocktail add-ons for $15–$20 per person and dessert add-ons for $15–$25 per person. Be sure to check our event calendar for availability during the first week of December: breakfast service is available Sunday, the 28th, from 5 AM–11 AM; full-day service on Monday, the 29th; lunch service from 10 AM–1 PM on Tuesday, the 30th through Thursday, the 2nd; and dinner service on Friday, the 3rd, from 4 PM–11 PM.
